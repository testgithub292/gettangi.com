Welcome to Tangi
Revolutionizing payments

Tangi is a FinTech company delivering secure, legal payment solutions tailored to the U.S. legal cannabis industry. We address the industry's challenges with limited access to traditional banking and card processing, offering a safe alternative to cash-only transactions for both retailers and consumers.

Tangi Payment Solution

Tangi offers a legal, compliant alternative to traditional payment systems for cannabis businesses. Our digital solution enables cannabis retailers to securely accept electronic payments, enhancing safety and convenience for everyone involved.

Tangi Token

The Tangi Token drives our payment ecosystem, enabling secure, incentivized transactions between retailers and consumers. With Tangi Tokens, retailers cut costs, while consumers unlock exclusive rewards and benefits.

Why Choose Tangi?

Tangi bridges gaps in traditional payment systems, meeting regulatory standards while fueling business growth. Our token model drives profitability, rewards consumers, and fosters sustainable impact.

Our Mission

At Tangi, we’re committed to empowering industries left behind by traditional banking, like cannabis, with secure and accessible financial solutions. We simplify payments for retailers, elevate convenience for consumers, and ensure a safer, fully compliant payment experience.

The Problem

The cannabis industry remains heavily cash-based because major banks and card networks don’t support it.
This reliance on cash creates security risks and logistical hurdles for over 8,000
retailers and 60 million customers. Tangi provides the solution.
